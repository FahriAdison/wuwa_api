API Features

  • Characters

    /characters
    /characters/:id

    Get information about Wuthering Waves characters, including stats, abilities, and more.

  • Weapons

    /weapons
    /weapons/:id

    Access data for all weapons in the game, including stats, abilities, and upgrade materials.

  • Echoes

    /echoes
    /echoes/:id

    Get information about Echoes, including effects, stats, and acquisition methods.

  • Attributes

    /attributes
    /attributes/:id

    Access data about character and weapon attributes in the game.

  • Sonatas

    /sonatas
    /sonatas/:id

    Get information about Sonatas, including effects and acquisition methods.

  • Stats

    /stats
    /stats/:id

    Access data about character and weapon stats in the game.

  • Substats

    /substats
    /substats/:id

    Get information about substats for equipment and characters.

  • Codes

    /codes
    /codes/:id

    Access redemption codes and rewards information.
